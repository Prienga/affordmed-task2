We Love All Animals

Animal welfare encompasses the physical and mental well-being of animals, focusing on their health, comfort, and
freedom from suffering, and is a core concern for animal activists and organizations.

Freedom from hunger and thirst

By ready access to fresh water and a diet to maintain full health and vigour.

Freedom from discomfort

By providing an appropriate environment including shelter and a comfortable resting area

Freedom from pain, injury or disease

By prevention through rapid diagnosis and treatment.

Freedom to express normal behaviour

By providing sufficient space, proper facilities and company of the animals own kind.

What are we doing now ?

Animal welfare is the well-being of non-human animals. Formal standards of animal welfare vary between contexts, but are debated mostly by animal welfare groups, legislators, and academics.

Our Core Values

1. Compassion

Treat all animals with kindness and respect.

2. Protection

Ensure animals are safeguarded from harm and cruelty.

3. Ethical Treatment

Promote humane practices in all interactions with animals.

4. Conservation

Preserve wildlife and their natural habitats.

5. Responsible Care

Provide proper food, shelter, and medical attention for animals.

6. Advocacy

Speak up for animal rights and promote stronger welfare laws.
